El Dorado

(Treasure Hunt)

“History always wins.”
In the bold pages of history, there lies hidden secrets and treasures, whose value surpasses even the wildest of your imaginations. The quest for glory demands sheer determination, rock-hard will and immense focus, as every move towards it is a choice of conviction. Only the best of the best survive, while the rest are forgotten and left behind. Can your perceptions take you all the way? Come find out if lady luck is truly on your side and claim the glory you truly deserve.

Rules and Regulations
1. Participants per team: 3
2. Use of vehicles will lead to immediate disqualification.
3. Unruly behavior with volunteers will not be tolerated.
4. Teams must follow the clues in the set order, skipping clues will lead to negative marking.
5. Teams will be provided with a total of 3 hints throughout the event.
6. Judge’s decision is final.
