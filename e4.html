Pehnava

(Fashion)

“When human contacts are so quick, fashion is an instant language’’. Each culture is distinct and has its charm. The power of a great outfit is impossible to overstate. At its best, fashion has the ability to transform your mood, identity and mostly your look. Dig the bottomless pit of creativity and represent your walk with the theme of ancient dynasties but with a modern fusion.

Rules and Regulations
1. The theme for the event is ‘ANCIENT DYNASTIES WITH A MODREN FUSION’
2. Number of participants: 10 + 2 members per team.
3. Time limit: 6+2 minutes exceeding the time limit will lead to negative marking.
4. Music must be submited two days prior to the event online or 2 hours offline.
5. It is mandatory for the teams to follow the theme.
6. Flammable props are strictly prohibited.
7. Judge’s decision is final.
