Virasat Ka Hunar

(Elysian’s got Talent)

“Everyone has talent. What’s rare is the courage to follow it to places where it leads.” Talent is the energy that keeps us going, that keeps us filled with meaning, happiness, excitement and anticipation. Passion is a powerful force in accomplishing anything you set your mind to and in experiencing work and life to the fullest extent possible. So buckle up, follow your gut and woo the audience with your scintillating performance.

Rules and Regulations
1. Event Type: Individual.
2. Participants can perform any form of creative activity they wish to present before the judges, there are no restrictions.
3. The time limit will be 2+1 minutes.
4. Vulgarity and obscene language will lead to disqualification.
5. Songs should be sent to the event heads two day prior to the event.
6. The judge’s decision will be final.

Event star: 5
